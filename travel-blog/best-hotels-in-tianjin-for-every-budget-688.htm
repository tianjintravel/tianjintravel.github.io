Best Hotels in Tianjin for Every Budget

  • Home
  • Travel Blog
  • -Blog Details

Tianjin, a bustling metropolis in northern China, is a city where history meets modernity. From colonial-era architecture to cutting-edge skyscrapers, Tianjin offers a unique blend of old and new. Whether you're a luxury traveler, a budget backpacker, or somewhere in between, the city has a hotel to suit your needs. Here’s a curated list of the best hotels in Tianjin for every budget.

Luxury Hotels in Tianjin

For those who want to indulge in opulence, Tianjin boasts several world-class luxury hotels that offer impeccable service, stunning views, and top-notch amenities.

The Ritz-Carlton, Tianjin

Located in the heart of the city, The Ritz-Carlton is synonymous with elegance. The hotel features spacious rooms with floor-to-ceiling windows overlooking the Hai River. Guests can enjoy the indoor pool, spa, and multiple fine-dining options, including a Cantonese restaurant and a rooftop bar.

St. Regis Tianjin

The St. Regis is another top-tier choice, known for its butler service and lavish interiors. The hotel’s signature afternoon tea is a must-try, and its proximity to the Tianjin Eye makes it ideal for sightseeing.

Mandarin Oriental, Tianjin

Blending contemporary design with traditional Chinese elements, the Mandarin Oriental offers a serene retreat. The spa is a highlight, offering treatments inspired by ancient Chinese medicine.

Mid-Range Hotels in Tianjin

If you’re looking for comfort without breaking the bank, these mid-range hotels provide excellent value for money.

Holiday Inn Tianjin Riverside

Situated along the Hai River, this hotel offers modern rooms with river views. The on-site restaurant serves both Western and Chinese cuisine, and the fitness center is well-equipped for travelers who like to stay active.

Crowne Plaza Tianjin Binhai

Perfect for business travelers, the Crowne Plaza in Binhai offers spacious rooms and a convenient location near the TEDA Convention Center. The executive lounge is a great perk for those who need a quiet place to work.

Novotel Tianjin Teda

A reliable choice for families, Novotel features a kids’ club and an outdoor pool. The hotel is close to the Binhai Aircraft Carrier Theme Park, making it a fun option for those traveling with children.

Budget-Friendly Hotels in Tianjin

Traveling on a tight budget? These affordable hotels don’t skimp on comfort or convenience.

Ibis Tianjin Railway Station

Ibis is a trusted name in budget accommodations, and this location is ideal for travelers arriving by train. The rooms are compact but clean, and the 24-hour snack bar is a lifesaver for late-night cravings.

GreenTree Inn Tianjin Italian Style Street

This hotel is a steal for its location near the Italian Style Street, a popular tourist spot. The rooms are basic but cozy, and the staff is known for being exceptionally friendly.

7 Days Inn Tianjin Binjiang Dao

For backpackers or solo travelers, 7 Days Inn offers no-frills lodging at unbeatable prices. The hotel is within walking distance of the Ancient Culture Street, so you can explore the city without spending much on transport.

Unique Stays in Tianjin

For those who want something out of the ordinary, Tianjin has a few quirky and unique accommodations worth considering.

Astor Hotel Tianjin

One of the oldest hotels in China, the Astor Hotel is a living piece of history. The colonial-era architecture and vintage decor make it a favorite among history buffs.

The Tangla Hotel Tianjin

This Tibetan-themed hotel offers a one-of-a-kind experience. From the intricate wood carvings to the traditional Tibetan artwork, every detail transports you to the Himalayas.

Moxy Tianjin Riverside

A trendy choice for millennials, Moxy combines affordability with a hip vibe. The communal spaces are designed for socializing, and the bar serves creative cocktails late into the night.

Family-Friendly Hotels in Tianjin

Traveling with kids? These hotels cater to families with amenities like pools, play areas, and spacious rooms.

Sheraton Tianjin Binhai Hotel

Sheraton’s family-friendly reputation holds true in Tianjin. The hotel offers connecting rooms, a children’s menu, and easy access to the Binhai New Area’s attractions.

Renaissance Tianjin Lakeview Hotel

Overlooking the scenic Tuanbo Lake, this hotel provides a peaceful setting for families. The indoor pool and kids’ club keep little ones entertained while parents relax.

InterContinental Tianjin

With its spacious suites and babysitting services, the InterContinental is a great choice for families who want a bit of luxury. The hotel’s location near the Tianjin Zoo is a bonus for animal-loving kids.

Business Hotels in Tianjin

For corporate travelers, these hotels offer excellent meeting facilities and convenient locations.

Shangri-La Hotel Tianjin

The Shangri-La is a favorite among business travelers for its state-of-the-art conference rooms and proximity to the Tianjin World Financial Center. The Horizon Club Lounge provides a quiet space for meetings or solo work.

Hyatt Regency Tianjin East

Located in the bustling Hedong District, the Hyatt Regency offers easy access to the Tianjin Railway Station. The hotel’s business center is open 24/7, catering to international travelers across time zones.

Pullman Tianjin Teda

Pullman’s modern design and tech-savvy amenities make it a hit with business guests. The hotel also offers a free shuttle service to the TEDA area, a hub for corporate offices.

Boutique Hotels in Tianjin

If you prefer intimate settings with personalized service, these boutique hotels are worth checking out.

The Lodge Hotel Tianjin

Inspired by European alpine lodges, this hotel offers a cozy atmosphere with fireplaces and wooden beams. The on-site restaurant specializes in hearty comfort food.

The Quincy Hotel Tianjin

A stylish boutique hotel with an artistic flair, The Quincy features rotating art exhibitions and a rooftop garden. The rooms are small but beautifully designed.

Hotel Indigo Tianjin Haihe

Located in the historic Five Great Avenues area, Hotel Indigo blends local culture with modern luxury. Each room is uniquely decorated with Tianjin-inspired motifs.

Pet-Friendly Hotels in Tianjin

Don’t want to leave your furry friend behind? These hotels welcome pets with open arms.

Aloft Tianjin Haihe

Aloft is known for its pet-friendly policies, and this location is no exception. The hotel even provides a special pet bed and treats for your four-legged companion.

Four Points by Sheraton Tianjin

Another Sheraton property that caters to pet owners, Four Points offers designated pet-walking areas and easy access to nearby parks.

Jinjiang Inn Tianjin Binjiang Dao

A budget-friendly option for pet lovers, Jinjiang Inn allows small pets and is located near the Hai River, perfect for morning walks.

Hotels Near Tianjin’s Top Attractions

If you want to stay close to the action, these hotels are ideally situated near Tianjin’s must-see spots.

Radisson Blu Hotel Tianjin

Just steps from the Ancient Culture Street, the Radisson Blu is perfect for tourists who want to immerse themselves in Tianjin’s heritage.

Sofitel Tianjin Riverside

Overlooking the Hai River and close to the Tianjin Eye, Sofitel offers stunning views and easy access to the city’s iconic landmarks.

Grand Mercure Tianjin Galaxy

Located in the shopping district of Binjiang Dao, this hotel is a shopper’s paradise, with the Galaxy Mall right next door.

Whether you’re splurging on a five-star experience or saving pennies for more adventures, Tianjin has a hotel to match your budget and style. From historic gems to modern marvels, the city’s accommodations are as diverse as its attractions.
