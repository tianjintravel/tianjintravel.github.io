Best Places to Play Pool & Darts in Tianjin at Night

  • Home
  • Travel Blog
  • -Blog Details

Tianjin, a vibrant metropolis blending historic charm with modern flair, offers an exciting nightlife scene for travelers and locals alike. If you’re looking to unwind with a game of pool or darts after exploring the city’s iconic landmarks, you’re in luck. From sleek sports bars to hidden speakeasies, here’s your ultimate guide to the best spots for pool and darts in Tianjin after dark.

The Top Pool Halls in Tianjin

1. The Rack Sports Bar & Lounge

Located in the heart of Tianjin’s downtown, The Rack is a favorite among expats and pool enthusiasts. With well-maintained tables, professional cues, and a lively atmosphere, it’s the perfect place to challenge friends or meet fellow players. The bar serves craft beers and cocktails, making it a great spot to socialize while you play.

Why Go?
- High-quality pool tables with regular tournaments.
- Happy hour deals on drinks.
- A mix of local and international crowds.

2. Billiard Palace (台球宫殿)

For a more upscale experience, head to Billiard Palace near Wu Da Dao. This venue boasts elegant decor, VIP rooms, and premium tables. It’s a popular choice for serious players who appreciate a refined setting.

Highlights:
- Private rooms for group events.
- Professional-grade equipment.
- Late-night hours (open until 2 AM).

3. Hustler’s Den

A hidden gem in the Hexi District, Hustler’s Den combines a dive-bar vibe with top-notch pool tables. The dim lighting and rock music create a laid-back atmosphere perfect for casual games.

Don’t Miss:
- Affordable hourly rates.
- Friendly staff who can offer tips for beginners.
- A no-frills, authentic pool hall experience.

Best Bars for Darts in Tianjin

1. Bullseye Darts Bar

As the name suggests, Bullseye is a haven for dart-throwing enthusiasts. Located near Italian Style Town, this bar features electronic dartboards, league nights, and a wide selection of imported beers.

What Makes It Special?
- State-of-the-art dartboards with scoring systems.
- Themed dart tournaments (e.g., “80s Night”).
- Cozy seating areas for spectators.

2. The Flying Arrow Pub

Nestled in the bustling Binjiang Dao area, The Flying Arrow Pub offers a British-style pub atmosphere with a focus on darts. The wooden decor and hearty pub food make it a great place to spend an evening.

Perks:
- Traditional steel-tip and soft-tip dartboards.
- Weekly trivia nights alongside dart games.
- A loyal expat community.

3. Darts & Drafts

This modern sports bar in Tianjin’s CBD is perfect for those who want to combine darts with other entertainment. Giant screens airing live sports, a wide beer selection, and multiple dartboards ensure there’s never a dull moment.

Why Visit?
- Interactive dart games with digital leaderboards.
- Craft beer pairings recommended by the bartenders.
- Spacious layout for large groups.

Unique Venues Combining Pool, Darts & More

1. The Games Hub

A one-stop entertainment spot in Tianjin, The Games Hub features pool tables, dartboards, arcade games, and even bowling lanes. It’s ideal for groups with diverse interests.

Standout Features:
- All-in-one gaming experience.
- Themed party nights (e.g., retro gaming tournaments).
- Food and drink packages for events.

2. Lucky Strike Tianjin

Part of the international chain, Lucky Strike offers a high-energy environment with luxury pool tables, electronic darts, and a full-service bar. The futuristic design and upbeat music make it a hotspot for night owls.

Extras:
- VIP areas with bottle service.
- Live DJ performances on weekends.
- Glow-in-the-dark dart games.

Tips for Enjoying Pool & Darts in Tianjin

  • Peak Hours: Most venues get busy after 9 PM, so arrive early to secure a table.
  • Etiquette: It’s common to challenge strangers to a friendly game, but always ask first.
  • Transport: Didi (China’s ride-hailing app) is the easiest way to get around at night.

Whether you’re a seasoned player or just looking for a fun night out, Tianjin’s pool and dart scene won’t disappoint. Grab a cue, aim for the bullseye, and dive into the city’s dynamic nightlife!
